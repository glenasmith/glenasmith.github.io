## Pull in the Libs

Add it to your pom.xml, build.gradle or [download it](https://code.google.com/p/guava-libraries/).


```xml
<dependency>
    <groupId>com.google.guava</groupId>
    <artifactId>guava</artifactId>
    <version>15.0</version>
</dependency>
```
